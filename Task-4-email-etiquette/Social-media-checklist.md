# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's
1. Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
2. Verify the credibility of information before sharing.
3. Maintain a professional and respectful tone in posts and comments.
4. Regularly review and update privacy settings.
5. Share content that positively reflects your academic or professional interests.

## Don'ts
1. Post sensitive personal information (e.g., home address, phone number).
2. Engage in cyberbullying or negative online arguments.
3. Share unverified rumors or fake news.
4. Overshare details about academic or workplace projects.
5. Use offensive language, inappropriate images, or copyrighted material without permission.
