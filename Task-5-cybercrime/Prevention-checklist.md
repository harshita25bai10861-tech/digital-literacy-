# Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students

1. Never click on links or download attachments from unknown or suspicious emails/messages.  
2. Verify website URLs before entering login credentials.  
3. Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication on all accounts.  
4. Avoid sharing UPI PIN, OTPs, or banking passwords with anyone.  
5. Use official bank/UPI apps downloaded from trusted sources only.  
6. Keep your devices and apps updated with the latest security patches.  
7. Log out from accounts after use, especially on shared/public devices.  
8. Educate yourself about common phishing tactics and red flags.  
9. Report cybercrime incidents immediately via:  
   - **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [https://www.cybercrime.gov.in](https://www.cybercrime.gov.in)  
   - **Helpline:** 1930
