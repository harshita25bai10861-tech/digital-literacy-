While researching phishing and online financial fraud, I was surprised by how easily even experienced users can be deceived by realistic-looking messages and fake websites. The level of sophistication in these attacks, such as using official logos, urgent language, and personal details, makes it difficult to detect scams. I also learned that college students are frequent targets because of their reliance on UPI apps and online banking for everyday transactions.  

One habit I will personally change is to **never share OTPs, UPI PINs, or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be bank officials**. I will also double-check website URLs and enable two-factor authentication for all financial apps to add an extra layer of security. Overall, this research emphasized the importance of digital vigilance and informed online behavior to prevent personal and financial losses.
